Sereno - Quo Warranto

Regina Iustitiae Sorority condemns the Supreme Court’s decision to grant the quo warranto petition against now former Chief Justice Maria Lourdes Sereno. 

It is at times like these that we, as members and students of the legal profession, must fight harder and speak louder to uphold the rule of law.

The 1987 Constitution embodies “The President, the Vice President, the Members of the Supreme Court, the Members of the Constitutional Commissions, and the Ombudsman may be removed from office on impeachment for, and conviction of, culpable violation of the Constitution, treason, bribery, graft and corruption, other high crimes, or betrayal of public trust.”

For the sake of justice, we cannot stand by and say, we 'MAY' uphold our Constitution. We 'MUST' uphold our Constitution and continue to fight for judicial independence.

We must continue to strive to uphold what is right and now, more than ever, our nation needs us to take a stand. We must not be afraid. We must continue to fight.

Regina Iustitiae Sorority
